THE PROBLEM

Banks are fighting yesterday's war

Synthetic identity fraud isn't identity theft—it's identity creation. Fraudsters combine real SSNs (often from children, elderly, or immigrants) with fabricated information to build entirely new "people" that pass traditional verification.

These synthetics are designed from the ground up to beat rule-based detection. They warm accounts for months, build legitimate-looking credit histories through authorized user tradelines, and exploit the gaps between how fraud ops thinks and how attackers operate.

By the time a synthetic busts out, they've extracted maximum credit across multiple institutions. The average loss per synthetic is $15,000. And your existing fraud stack probably can't see them coming.

CPN/ITIN Fraud

Tradeline Manipulation

Authorized user stuffing (6+ AU accounts), impossible credit velocity (high score + thin file), and piggybacking patterns from tradeline mills.

Detection of identities being "warmed" across data aggregators, rewards programs, and utility accounts to establish false legitimacy.

Application Velocity

05

Bust-Out Indicators
